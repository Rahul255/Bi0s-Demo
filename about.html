ABOUT US

Team bi0s from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

Team bi0s from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a well-established group of computer security enthusiasts focusing on various areas including but not limited to web application security, computer forensics, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, hardware security, cryptography and Android security. bi0s is largely a CTF team actively taking part in the international CTFs round the year besides carrying out research on security issues in the real world. Fuelled by the urge for community service, bi0s has been organizing workshops and educating the masses about the need for cyber security.

CRYPTO

Deciphering highly encrypted messages by cracking complex algorithms.

HARDWARE

A group of hardware enthusiasts breaking and making electronics, perhaps anything with solder in it.

REVERSE ENGINEERING

A group of insane people who crack binaries apart 1 bit at a time to uncover the hidden secrets.

FORENSICS

Unravelling the mysteries of the footprints left behind by the attacks.
